Our Mission

"Every college student in India deserves a shot at real-world work — not just internship applications that go nowhere."

Skill Linkr exists to bridge the gap between talented students and businesses that need affordable, quality work. We believe that your college years shouldn't just be about attending classes — they should be about building skills, earning money, and gaining experience that actually matters.

We started right here in Uttar Pradesh, and we're building for every student in every tier-2 and tier-3 city who never had access to the right opportunities.

How We Started

A college frustration turned into a platform

We were B.Tech students at AKTU who could design, code, and write — but couldn't find anyone willing to pay us for it. Every internship wanted "experience". Every platform was built for full-time professionals.

So we built Skill Linkr. A platform that takes students seriously, connects them with real clients, and makes sure they get paid — securely, fairly, and fast.

What We Stand For

Our core values

🎯

Students First

Every decision we make starts with one question: is this good for students? Their growth, earnings, and experience always come before everything else.

🔒

Trust & Safety

Escrow-protected payments, verified student profiles, and transparent processes — we make sure no one gets cheated, ever.

🌍

Tier-2 India First

We're built for students in Agra, Meerut, Kanpur, Lucknow — not just metros. Opportunity shouldn't depend on your postcode.

⚡

Speed & Simplicity

Post a project in 5 minutes. Apply in 2. No bloated dashboards, no confusing flows. We respect everyone's time.

❤️

Real Relationships

We're not just a marketplace. We want students to build long-term relationships with clients — repeat work, references, and real careers.

📈

Grow Together

When students earn more and clients get better work, everyone wins. We measure success by how much our community grows — not just our revenue.
